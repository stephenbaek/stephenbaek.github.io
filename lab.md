# About Us
The Visual Intelligence Laboratory @ UVA (the "VIVA Lab") is a research group directed by Professor Stephen Baek at the University of Virginia School of Data Science. We tackle both fundamental and applied research problems in **geometric data analysis** to understand the roles of geometry in various scientific phenomena. Interested students, researchers, or any other individuals are always welcome to [contact us](/contact)!

# Join the Lab

We are always looking for **highly motivated students and postdocs** interested in working on challenging problems at the intersection of AI and physical systems.

## What We Look For
- Curiosity and initiative
- Strong foundation in **math (vector calculus, differential equations) and/or physics**
- Experience with **programming (Python, PyTorch/TensorFlow, MATLAB, etc.)**
- Ability to work on **open-ended, research-driven problems**

## How to Apply

### Postdocs
- Email me directly

### PhD Students
- Apply through the [official university application](https://applycentral.virginia.edu/apply/) process
- Choose between [Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering](https://engineering.virginia.edu/departments/mechanical-and-aerospace-engineering/apply/mae-graduate-admissions) and [Data Science](https://datascience.virginia.edu/degrees/phd), depending on your interests
- Mention my name in your application

### MS / Undergraduate Researchers
- Limited internship positions available each semester  
- Preference given to students with:
  - Strong commitment to research (remember, research internships require significant time and effort, on top of your coursework)
  - Demonstrated work ethics
  - Strong math & physics background

### How to Reach Out

If you are interested in joining, please send me a *concise* email including:
- CV / resume  
- Brief description of your background  
- Specific research interests  
- (Optional) links to projects / GitHub

📩 baek (at) virginia (dot) edu

**Due to the volume of emails I receive everyday, you may not receive a response.**
