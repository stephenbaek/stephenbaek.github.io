I collaborate with organizations at the intersection of AI, 3D computer vision, physics, and real-world systems, to help teams move from ideas to working solutions.

Whether you're building next-generation AI products, exploring physics-informed models, or translating research into deployable systems, I can help you accelerate progress and avoid costly dead ends.

I work with companies and research teams on:

- AI strategy and technical direction
- Computer vision and multimodal systems
- Physics-informed machine learning and simulation
- Sports analytics and human motion modeling
- Rapid prototyping of high-risk, high-reward ideas
- Other client-tailored AI projects

Typical engagements include:

- Technical advisory
- Custom AI model/software development
- Deep-dive problem solving sessions
- Prototype development and validation
- Research translation → product strategy
